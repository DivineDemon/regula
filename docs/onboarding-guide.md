# Regula Onboarding Guide

Complete step-by-step guide to onboarding your organization on Regula.

## Overview

The Regula onboarding process is an 8-step wizard that collects comprehensive information about your fintech company and uses AI to automatically discover relevant regulatory monitoring targets. The entire process takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.

## Prerequisites

Before starting onboarding, ensure you have:
- ✅ Created an account and verified your email
- ✅ Created or joined an organization
- ✅ Gathered company information (registration details, services, countries of operation)

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1: Company Profile

In this step, you'll provide basic information about your company.

**Required Fields:**
- **Legal Entity Name**: Your company's registered legal name
- **Country of Incorporation**: The country where your company is legally registered (ISO country code)
- **Primary Jurisdiction**: Your main regulatory jurisdiction
- **Fintech Category**: Select from:
  - EMI (Electronic Money Institution)
  - Neobank
  - PSP (Payment Service Provider)
  - Remittance
  - Cryptocurrency
  - Lending
  - Investment
  - Insurance
  - Wealth Management
  - Trading Platform
  - Other
- **Business Model**: Choose B2C, B2B, or B2B2C

**Optional Fields:**
- Trading Name (if different from legal name)
- Company Registration Number
- Date of Incorporation
- Website URL
- Company Size (startup, small, medium, large, enterprise)

**Tips:**
- Use your official registered company name
- Select the fintech category that best describes your primary business
- The primary jurisdiction is where most of your regulatory obligations are

### Step 2: Services & Products

Select all the services and products your company offers.

**Available Services:**
- Money Transfer (domestic and cross-border)
- Payment Processing
- Card Issuance (debit/credit cards)
- Wallet Services (digital wallets)
- Remittance Services
- Foreign Exchange (FX) Services
- Cryptocurrency Exchange
- Cryptocurrency Wallet
- Lending Services
- Investment Platform
- Savings Account Services
- Current Account Services
- Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)
- Crowdfunding Platform
- Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Lending
- Robo-Advisory Services
- Insurance Distribution
- Other

**Tips:**
- Select all services you offer, even if they're secondary
- More services selected = better target discovery
- You can add service-specific details for some services

### Step 3: Geographic Operations

Define where your company operates and how.

**For Each Country:**
1. **Select Country**: Choose from the country selector
2. **Operation Type**: 
   - **Direct**: You have a licensed entity in this country
   - **Indirect**: You operate through partners/agents
   - **Data Processing**: You only process data in this country
3. **Services Offered**: Select which services you offer in this country (from Step 2)
4. **License Status**:
   - Licensed (fully licensed)
   - Applying (license application in progress)
   - Exempt (exempt from licensing)
   - Not Required (license not required)
   - Unlicensed (operating without license)
5. **License Details** (if licensed):
   - License Number
   - Issuing Authority
   - Issue Date
   - Expiry Date (if applicable)
   - Capital Requirement (if applicable)

**Tips:**
- Add all countries where you have operations, even if indirect
- Be accurate about license status for compliance
- Include countries where you plan to expand soon

### Step 4: Compliance Mapping

Map compliance requirements to your service-country combinations.

**Compliance Matrix:**
For each service-country combination, select applicable compliance requirements:
- AML (Anti-Money Laundering)
- KYC (Know Your Customer)
- CTF (Counter-Terrorism Financing)
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
- PSD2 (Payment Services Directive 2)
- EMI License
- PSP License
- Banking License
- Remittance License
- Crypto License
- Securities License
- Insurance License
- Data Protection
- Consumer Protection
- Capital Requirements
- Reporting Requirements
- Audit Requirements
- Other

**Compliance Framework:**
- AML Framework Name
- KYC Procedures Description
- Data Protection Framework
- Privacy Policy URL
- Terms of Service URL
- Regulatory Certifications (checkboxes):
  - PCI DSS
  - ISO 27001
  - ISO 9001
  - SOC 2
  - Other

**Tips:**
- The matrix is auto-populated from your services and countries
- Add context notes for complex compliance situations
- Be thorough - this helps with target discovery

### Step 5: Partnerships

Provide information about your key partnerships.

**Partnership Types:**
1. **Banking Partners**: List your banking partners (multi-input)
2. **Payment Networks**: Select from:
   - Visa
   - Mastercard
   - American Express
   - Other
3. **Payment System Integrations**: Select from:
   - Raast (Pakistan)
   - UPI (India)
   - Mobile Money
   - Other
4. **Remittance Partners**:
   - Number of partners
   - Major corridors (select countries)
5. **Technology Partners**: List technology partners
6. **Third-Party Service Providers**: Select In-house or Outsourced

**Tips:**
- Focus on major partnerships that affect your regulatory obligations
- Remittance corridors help identify relevant cross-border regulations

### Step 6: Review & Submit

Review all the information you've provided before submitting.

**Review Sections:**
- Company Information
- Services & Products
- Geographic Operations
- Compliance Mapping
- Partnerships

**Actions:**
- ✅ Review each section carefully
- ✏️ Click "Edit" on any section to go back and make changes
- 📄 Use "Print Summary" to save a copy (optional)
- ➡️ Click "Submit" to save your profile and proceed to target discovery

**Tips:**
- Take time to review - accurate information leads to better target suggestions
- You can always update your profile later from settings

### Step 7: Target Discovery

AI analyzes your profile to discover relevant regulatory targets.

**What Happens:**
1. Your profile is sent to the AI service
2. The AI analyzes your services, countries, and compliance requirements
3. Relevant regulatory websites and sources are identified
4. Targets are ranked by relevance and confidence

**During Discovery:**
- ⏳ Progress indicator shows discovery status
- ⏱️ Estimated time: 30-60 seconds
- 🔄 Automatic retry on failure

**If Discovery Fails:**
- You can retry the discovery
- Or proceed to manually add targets in Step 8

**Tips:**
- Be patient - AI analysis takes time
- If discovery fails, you can still add targets manually

### Step 8: Target Selection

Review and select the discovered regulatory targets.

**Target Information:**
Each target shows:
- **URL**: The regulatory source URL
- **Label**: Suggested name for the target
- **Jurisdiction**: Country/jurisdiction
- **Category**: Regulatory category (AML, KYC, licensing, etc.)
- **Confidence Score**: AI confidence (0-1)
- **Reasoning**: Why this target is relevant
- **Relevant Services**: Services that make this target relevant
- **Relevant Countries**: Countries that make this target relevant

**Actions:**
- ☑️ Select/deselect individual targets
- ☑️ "Select All" / "Deselect All"
- 🔍 Search/filter targets
- 📊 Sort by confidence, jurisdiction, or category
- ➕ Add targets manually (if needed)
- ➡️ "Create Selected Targets" to proceed

**Tips:**
- Review the reasoning for each target
- Higher confidence targets are usually more relevant
- You can add more targets later from the Targets page
- Don't select targets you already monitor elsewhere

## After Onboarding

Once onboarding is complete:

1. **Profile Saved**: Your organization profile is saved and can be updated anytime
2. **Targets Created**: Selected targets are automatically created and configured
3. **Initial Crawl**: First crawl is triggered for all selected targets
4. **Dashboard Access**: You're redirected to the dashboard

## Updating Your Profile

You can update your organization profile at any time:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Organization**
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Update any section
4. Save changes

## Troubleshooting

### Can't proceed to next step?
- Check for validation errors (red text under fields)
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check your internet connection

### Target discovery failed?
- Check your internet connection
- Try again (click "Retry")
- Add targets manually in Step 8
- Contact support if issues persist

### Lost progress?
- Your progress is auto-saved to browser storage
- Refresh the page to restore saved data
- If you clear browser data, you'll need to start over

### Need to start over?
- Clear your browser's localStorage
- Or contact support to reset your onboarding

## Best Practices

1. **Be Accurate**: Provide accurate information for better target suggestions
2. **Be Complete**: Fill out all relevant sections, even optional ones
3. **Be Specific**: More details = better AI analysis
4. **Review Carefully**: Take time in the review step
5. **Select Wisely**: Only select targets you'll actually monitor
