A Real-Time Regulatory Intelligence Platform for Emerging-Market FinTechs
🧩 1. Problem Statement
Regulated businesses—especially FinTech startups and SMBs in emerging 
markets—face a critical and growing challenge:
1.1 Regulatory changes are constant, fragmented, and 
unpredictable
Regulators publish updates across:
Scattered websites
PDFs and circulars
Notices buried in archives
Non-standard formatting
Inconsistent publication schedules
FinTech operators must track multiple regulators at once (central banks, 
securities commissions, SECP, SBP, etc.). Missing an update can lead to serious 
consequences.
1.2 Compliance burden is heavy and expensive
Most SMBs do not have a dedicated compliance or legal team. Instead, they rely 
on:
Manual checking of regulator websites
Outdated mailing lists

🔍 3. How Regula Works
1. User selects their jurisdictions and regulators.
2. Regula automatically monitors each source in real-time.
3. Any update (HTML, PDF, circular, notification) triggers:
Change detection
AI summary
Classification (AML, KYC, licensing, etc.)
Impact scoring
4. Only important, actionable alerts are sent to the user.
5. Users can assign, triage, track, and close alerts—creating an audit trail.
This shifts compliance from manual monitoring → automated intelligence.
